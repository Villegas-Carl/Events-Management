## About The Project

Carl&KD Events Management is a comprehensive event planning and coordination platform that helps clients create unforgettable events. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we connect you with the best suppliers and manage every detail of your special day.

### Our Mission
To transform your vision into reality by providing seamless event coordination and access to premium event suppliers, ensuring every moment is perfect.

## eatures

-  **Event Planning & Coordination** - Full-service event management from concept to execution
- **Supplier Network** - Curated list of trusted vendors including:
  - Caterers & Food Services
  - Photographers & Videographers
  - Florists & Decorators
  - Venues & Locations
  - Entertainment & Musicians
  - Wedding Planners & Coordinators
-  **Event Dashboard** - Track all your events in one place
-  **Responsive Design** - Access on desktop, tablet, or mobile
-  **Secure Booking System** - Safe and reliable reservation process
